江戸時間

Live your day on Edo period time

Before clocks divided the day into equal hours, Japan used 不定時法 (futei-jihō) — a variable hour system where daytime and nighttime each split into six periods that stretched and compressed with the seasons.

Edo Time brings this 400-year-old timekeeping to your iPhone. Using your location and precise solar calculations, see the current 時（とき）as it was experienced in Edo-period Japan.
