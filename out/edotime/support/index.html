FAQ

Why does the time look different from my clock?
Edo Time uses the traditional Japanese variable hour system (不定時法). Hours change length with the seasons based on sunrise and sunset at your location.

Does it need internet?
No. Solar calculations are performed entirely offline using your location.

Why does it need my location?
Sunrise and sunset times depend on where you are. Your location is used only for solar calculations and is never sent to any server.

Privacy

Edo Time does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Location data is used on-device only for sunrise/sunset calculations. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts.
